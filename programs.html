Our Programs

Creating lasting change in education, empowerment, and health

📚 Education Support

Our Education Support program aims to break the cycle of poverty through quality education. We provide scholarships, learning materials, and mentorship to underprivileged children in rural areas.

What we do: Scholarship distribution, School supply donations, Tuition support, Digital literacy training

Beneficiaries: 2,500+ children

Communities: 25 villages

Annual Budget: ₹15,00,000

Impact Metric: 95% school retention rate

👩‍💼 Women Empowerment

We believe in empowering women to become economically independent and socially confident. Our program provides vocational training, microfinance support, and business development guidance.

What we do: Skill training programs, Financial literacy, Microfinance loans, Self-help group formation

Beneficiaries: 1,200+ women

Communities: 15 villages

Annual Budget: ₹12,00,000

Impact Metric: 80% started own business

🏥 Community Health

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We organize medical camps, health awareness sessions, and provide basic medical support to remote communities with limited healthcare infrastructure.

What we do: Medical camps, Health awareness, Vaccination drives, Health insurance assistance

Beneficiaries: 8,000+ people

Communities: 30 villages

Annual Budget: ₹10,00,000

Impact Metric: 5,000+ medical consultations
