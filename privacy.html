Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and donation details when you donate, volunteer, or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to process your donations, send receipts, communicate with volunteers, and provide updates about our programs.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, and disclosure.

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to external parties. We may share information with payment processors and email service providers to fulfill our services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@bharathnavayu va.org
