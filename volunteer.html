❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience?

No, we welcome volunteers from all backgrounds. We provide training and support to help you succeed.

How much time commitment is required?

It depends on the volunteer role. Some opportunities require 4-6 hours per week, while others are more flexible.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes, we have several remote volunteer positions available in areas like social media management, fundraising, and technology support.

Is there a minimum duration?

We recommend volunteering for at least 3 months to have meaningful impact, but we're flexible for special projects.
