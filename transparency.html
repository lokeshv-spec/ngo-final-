Our Commitment to Transparency

At Spandanaforchange, we believe that transparency is the foundation of trust. Every rupee donated by our supporters is accounted for, tracked, and reported. We are committed to:

  • ✓ Publishing detailed financial reports quarterly and annually
  • ✓ Maintaining 80-85% direct program spend ratio
  • ✓ Regular audits by certified independent auditors
  • ✓ Making all donor information and impact data publicly available
