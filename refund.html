Refund Policy

⚠️ Important: All donations to BHARATH NAVA YUVA ASSOCIATION are voluntary and non-refundable in normal circumstances.

1. General Refund Policy

Donations made to BHARATH NAVA YUVA ASSOCIATION are voluntary contributions and are generally non-refundable. Once processed, donations are committed to our programs and projects.

2. Exceptional Refund Circumstances

Refunds may be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Duplicate transactions or accidental double charges
  • Transaction reversal at the donor's request within 7 days of donation
  • Technical errors or payment processing failures
  • Payment fraud or unauthorized transactions

3. Refund Process

To request a refund for the above reasons:

  • Contact us within 7 days of the donation
  • Provide transaction details and reason for refund request
  • We will review and respond within 10 business days
  • Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method

4. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will typically be processed within 5-7 business days. However, depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3-5 business days for the amount to appear in your account.

5. Non-Refundable Items

  • Regular donations committed to specific programs
  • Donations made with explicit program designation
  • Monthly recurring donation commitments

6. Contact for Refund Requests

For refund requests or inquiries, please contact:
refunds@bharathnavayuva.org
Phone: +91-78459-61234

7. Changes to Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website.
